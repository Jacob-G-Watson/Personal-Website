Public Speaking

University

Working as a Student Ambassador for my university was one of the most fulfilling and valuable experiences I had during my academic journey. The contracted role allowed me to develop and refine my presentation skills and facilitation skills to an exceptional level of quality and effectiveness. I took great pleasure in engaging with various audiences, including prospective students, parents, teachers, and university staff, and conveying my passion and knowledge for the university and its programs. I also benefited from listening to their diverse perspectives and inquiries, which challenged me to think critically and creatively about the university and its offerings.

After University

Public speaking is one of my passions and strengths that I have cultivated since my time as a Student Ambassador. I enjoy sharing my views and experiences on topics that interest me, as well as learning from different viewpoints and perspectives of others. I have a curious and humble mindset that drives me to explore various fields of knowledge and acknowledge the limits of my own understanding. I always welcome opportunities to have meaningful and constructive discussions with diverse audiences. In the past year, I have been involved in several public speaking engagements related to my current company and industry. I have visited high school classrooms and delivered presentations on the information technology sector and its career prospects. I have also participated in panel discussions with other experts and professionals in the field, where we exchanged insights and opinions on the latest trends and challenges in the industry.
